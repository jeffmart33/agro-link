Private (Individual) Account

This account is best for you, if...

  • You are an individual looking to BUY.
  • You are an individual wanting to SELL pre-loved or pre-owned items.
  • You are an individual looking to FIND a Job or Service.
  • You want to use ‘CHAT’ to get in touch with interested buyers or sellers.
  • You want access to your Favourite ads and Saved searches across devices.
  • You want to easily EDIT or DELETE your ads.

PRO Niaga (Business) Account

This account is best for you, if...

  • You are a Professional Seller and/or Agent and/or Business Owner (with or without a registered company).
  • You want a personalised dashboard for easy monitoring, managing and promoting of your ads.
  • You want an online store front with your own personalised URL.
  • You will get an online store front with your own personalised URL linked to ONE category.
  • You want to add multiple users to manage your account.
